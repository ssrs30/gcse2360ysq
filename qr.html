How to use:
1. Deploy this folder to Netlify Drop (drag & drop, free)
2. Copy the URL Netlify gives you (e.g. https://xxx.netlify.app)
3. Open qr.html on the deployed site, paste the URL, and generate the QR code
4. Print or display the QR code — others scan it to take the quiz
